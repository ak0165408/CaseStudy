Why is CIS important?

The Computer Information Sciences or "CIS" division,

is a growing field of academics that has become incredibly essential in today's virtual world.

The CIS division is responsible for spearheading the industry of website development and program coding.

Many times every day we use programs that were designed by someone in the CIS division. Simply using your toaster to make some toast for breakfast in the morning would require a program to know when to eject the toast and even how long it should be toasted!
